Protecting NZ’s Native Birds

-New Zealand’s native birds are under threat from habitat loss and predators.
We must act now to protect them for future generations.

New Zealand is home to many unique native birds.
Unfortunately there are various threats to these native birds due to habitat destruction, predators, and climate change. It is crucial that we take action to protect these birds and their habitats. Because at the rate we are going in, many of these unique native birds could become extinct in the near future. Learning about these birds and how we can protect them could change these unique native birds lifes and make New Zealand a wonderful place to be in!
